📊 Bike Sales Power BI Dashboard
This Power BI report analyzes bike sales performance across different dimensions like customer demographics, product categories, regions, and revenue trends. It's designed to give stakeholders a quick overview of business performance with interactive visuals.

🧩 Key Features
🚴‍♂️ Sales by Product Category
Breakdown of total sales and units sold by bike type.

🌍 Sales by Region
Interactive map and bar charts showing regional performance.

👤 Customer Demographics
Gender, age group, and income level insights.

📈 Time-Based Trends
Monthly and yearly sales performance trends.

📦 Inventory and Profitability
Product-level metrics on stock status and gross profit.
